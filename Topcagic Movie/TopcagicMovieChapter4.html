Predator

Movie Description

Dutch (Arnold Schwarzenegger), a soldier of fortune, is hired by the U.S. government to secretly rescue a group of politicians trapped in Guatemala.
But when Dutch and his team, which includes weapons expert Blain (Jesse Ventura) and CIA agent George (Carl Weathers), land in Central America, something is gravely wrong.
After finding a string of dead bodies, the crew discovers they are being hunted by a brutal creature with superhuman strength and the ability to disappear into its surroundings.

A little more information on the world of Predator:

Since the release of the first Predator movie in 1987, several more movies, like the new 2019 release of Predator, have came out with just as many crossovers into other universes.
Predator vs Alien being the most popular of these crossovers to the point of being canon part of the Predator-Alien story. 3 cross over movies have been released pitting the ultimate hunters vs the ultimate killer.
Another crossover that was popular is Batman vs Predator featuring a rogue predator out after Gotham's best detective. 3 Predators would join Batman on the hunt after this other rogue Predator.

People/Creatures the Predator has faced off with:

  • Alien
  • Batman
  • Terminator
  • Dinosaurs
  • Judge Dredd
  • Ghost
  • Witchblade
  • Darkness
  • More then likely any big beast in our real world
