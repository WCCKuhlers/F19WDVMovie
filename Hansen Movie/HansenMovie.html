Synopsis

The Top Gun Naval Fighter Weapons School is where the best of the best
train to refine their elite flying skills. When hotshot fighter pilot Maverick
is sent to the school, his reckless attitude and cocky demeanor
put him at odds with the other pilots, especially the cool and collected Iceman.
But Maverick isn't only competing to be the top fighter pilot, he's
also fighting for the attention of his beautiful flight instructor, Charlotte
Blackwood.
